YEAR 1 SEMESTER 2

Streamed into EEE, the modules that I took, and my opinions on them :)

Course Content

EE1002 Physics Foundation for EEE

Surprisingly easier than the previous module that I took for Physics in Semester 1, but learned new topics such as quantum physics and optics which were pretty interesting :)

Course Content

MH1811 Mathematics 2

Open book exam :) Unfortunately, it is kind of essential because there were too many formulas and stuff to remember here. Quizzes were also pretty hard as compared to last semester's mathematics.

Course Content

EG0001 Engineers and Society

What I would describe this module is like Social Studies, where we would learn Singapore's history and how she overcame challenges under the leadership in Singapore. A pretty interesting module overall.

Course Content

EE1071 EEE Laboratory 1

A laboratory module which teaches us the stuff used in a typical electrical laboratory which involves the soldering of circuits and learning about the breadboard.

Course Content

EE1003 Introduction To Materials For Electronics

Chemistry. Never thought I will be actually studying mole concepts again haha. Learned new stuff such as band gaps and quantum states here too, which will be essential for Year 2 modules :)

Course Content

EE1005 From Computational Thinking To Programming

C Programming in a nutshell. From basic stuff such as declaring variable to loops to arrays/pointers. Looking forward to Python though :)

Course Content

EE8092 Digital Lifestyle

The stuff we used in everyday lives can be learned in this module. While it is certainly very content heavy, you will come to learn the different gadgets we used and come to appreciate it.
